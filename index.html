What is CivicSnap?

CivicSnap is a mobile app that bridge the reporting gaps and bonds citizens, authorities, and volunteers onto a single, trusted platform.

By sharing real-time reports and alerts, we build a connected community dedicated to mutual safety and civic accountability for a better homeland.

The Problem

Our communities face significant challenges in reporting and addressing civic issues.

The Reporting Gap

Many citizens face crimes, hazards, and civic issues but have no single, accessible platform to report them. This gap means countless incidents remain invisible to authorities.

Widespread Infrastructure Issues

The country faces persistent challenges like floods and fires. A lack of a unified system to map these problems in real-time makes it difficult to prioritize responses.

Broken Reporting Process

Traditional methods are often slow, bureaucratic, and lack transparency. Citizens cannot easily track their complaints, leading to frustration and a loss of trust.

Unreported Problems

Many issues, particularly local crimes or corruption, go unreported due to fear or complex procedures. This creates a "dark figure" of crime and dysfunction.

The Solution

Civic-Snap is revolutionizing civic engagement by streamlining the reporting process for community issues.

  • - This intuitive app not only simplifies reporting but also ensures that problems are efficiently addressed by goverment agencies.
  • - It acts as a tool for addressing social issues and to pressurize goverment agencies to solve them.

How It Works

The 1 Minute Report

1

SNAP

The user takes a quick photo or video of the issue at hand with just a tap of a button.

2

Describe

User describe the issue in our description box in a few words.

3

Categories

By reading the description our AI will be able to automatically choose the right category or people can select it manually.

4

SUBMIT

Finally, the automatically created report is sent directly to the relevant department with location, such as City Corporation, without the user needing to navigate bureaucratic channels.

Traditional Reporting Apps

Traditional reporting apps require users to navigate complex forms and lengthy processes to submit issues. Citizens often abandon the task due to confusion, frustration, and the demand for detailed information, leading to underreporting of critical community problems.

Civic-Snap

Civic-Snap revolutionizes this process by allowing users to simply point and shoot, dramatically reducing the cognitive load. Our AI handles the bureaucratic intricacies, routing reports directly to the appropriate departments without user intervention, creating a seamless and efficient reporting experience.

Growth & Future Vision

Civic-Snap's roadmap for enhancing urban reporting and community engagement.

Phase 1 (Now)

Prove the AI-reporting concept for 3-4 categories in Dhaka, validating the technology and gathering user feedback.

Phase 2 (B2G SaaS)

Develop and sell a premium SaaS Dashboard to goverment agencies, enabling real-time, AI-prioritized issue tracking for goverment officials.

Phase 3 (Gamification)

Introduce a "Civic Leaderboard" that rewards active citizens with points redeemable at local shops, fostering community involvement.

Phase 4 (Expansion)

Scale Civic-Snap to additional goverment agencies, broadening the reporting categories and AI involvement.
